Stocks Simulator turorial: Introduction


Part 0 : Introduction

For many new investors, investing in the stock market can be a daunting and complex task. Investment has brought considerable rewards and risks. So why not use virtual accounts to practice before the hard savings in the future?

Our simulator uses real data from the stock market to recreate the experience of using real online brokerage accounts. Virtual cash balances are provided to users, and virtual transactions can be conducted using real market data. There are many uses of the simulator: educators use it as a tool to introduce the stock market to students. Some are used to test new investment strategies, and others use it to experience the "feeling" of the transaction. Whatever your current financial knowledge level, simulators can help you become better investors.
